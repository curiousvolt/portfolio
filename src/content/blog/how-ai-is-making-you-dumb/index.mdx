# How AI is Making You Dumb

We live in a magical time where the sum total of human knowledge is not only accessible in our pockets, but a sophisticated algorithm will actually synthesize it for us. But there's a catch.

## Outsourcing Our Brains
When you rely on ChatGPT to write your emails, code your scripts, and summarize your reading, you're doing more than just saving time. You're outsourcing the metabolic work of thinking. The brain is like a muscle—if you don't use it, you lose it. The small acts of struggle to find a word, to construct an argument, or to debug a loop are what build deep neural pathways.

## The Attention Economy
It goes beyond just prompt generation. AI-driven recommendation feeds spoon-feed us content that requires zero analytical friction. Over time, our attention spans decrease, and our capacity for deep, deliberate thinking diminishes.

## Reclaiming Your Mind
How do we fix this? Simply put, use AI as a tool, not a crutch. Do the first draft yourself. Solve the bug before you paste it into the chat. Embrace the struggle of thinking, because that friction is where true learning happens.
